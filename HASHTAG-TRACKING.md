When sharing links with #3mpwrApp, use these UTM parameters to track traffic:


## üìà Key Metrics to Track

### Engagement Metrics
- **Total Mentions:** Number of times #3mpwrApp is used
- **Reach:** Total potential audience exposed to the hashtag
- **Impressions:** Number of times hashtag was displayed
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes, comments, shares per post

### Sentiment Analysis
- **Positive:** Supportive, enthusiastic mentions
- **Neutral:** Informational, factual mentions
- **Negative:** Critical or complaint mentions

### User Demographics
- **Top Contributors:** Users posting most frequently with hashtag
- **Geographic Distribution:** Where posts originate
- **Peak Times:** When hashtag is most active

### Content Types
- **Events:** Posts about community events
- **Advocacy:** Posts about disability/worker rights
- **Resources:** Posts sharing helpful information
- **Community Stories:** Personal experiences shared
