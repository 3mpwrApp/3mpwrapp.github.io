## Known Limitations

**We practice radical transparency about accessibility gaps:**

### Current Limitations:

**Third-Party Content:**
- Some embedded social media widgets (Facebook, Twitter) may not meet our standards
- Working with platforms to improve accessibility
- Always provide text alternatives

**Legacy Content:**
- Some older blog posts from 2024 may have minor accessibility issues
- Active audit and remediation project ongoing
- All new content fully accessible from October 2025 onward

**PDF Documents:**
- Not all downloadable PDFs are fully tagged/accessible yet
- Priority: Legal templates and guides (completed)
- Ongoing: Blog post PDFs, resource downloads
- Timeline: 100% accessible PDFs by Q1 2026

**Dynamic Features:**
- Some JavaScript-powered features require JavaScript enabled
- Fallbacks provided for critical functionality (forms, navigation)
- Progressive enhancement approach ensures basic access

**Browser Compatibility:**
- Optimized for modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Limited testing on IE11 (end of life, <1% usage)
- Mobile browsers fully supported
