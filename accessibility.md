#### Text Customization

- [Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2](https://www.w3.org/WAI/WCAG22/quickref/)- **Font Size**: Adjustable from 90% to 150%

- [Section 508](https://www.section508.gov/)- **Letter Spacing**: Normal or wide

- [Canadian Accessibility Act](https://www.canada.ca/en/employment-social-development/programs/accessible-canada.html)- **Font Family**: Default, Readable, or Dyslexia-friendly
