## 💡 What Beta Testers Do

**You don't need technical skills!** We need testers of all abilities and backgrounds.

### Your Role:

✅ **Use the App**
- Try features in real-world situations
- Use it like you would any app
- Explore at your own pace

✅ **Share Feedback**
- What works well?
- What's confusing?
- What features do you wish existed?
- How's the accessibility?

✅ **Report Issues**
- Bugs (things that don't work)
- Crashes or freezes
- Accessibility barriers
- Privacy concerns

✅ **Help Improve**
- Suggest new features
- Test fixes for reported issues
- Share your disability perspective
- Help shape the roadmap

## 📋 Requirements

### Minimum Requirements:

**Device:**
- iOS 14.0+ (iPhone, iPad)
- OR Android 8.0+ (phones, tablets)
- Internet connection (for initial download and cloud features)

**Time Commitment:**
- As much or as little as you want!
- No minimum usage required
- Feedback surveys optional (but appreciated)

**Skills:**
- None! Just be yourself and use the app
- No technical knowledge required
- All abilities welcome

**Language:**
- English, French, or 6 Indigenous languages supported
- Screen reader users encouraged to apply

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Is beta testing really free?</strong></summary>

**YES!** Beta testing is 100% free. The full app will also be 100% free forever - no catch, no paid tiers, no subscriptions.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How many people are you accepting?</strong></summary>

**Phase 1 Closed Beta:** We're accepting testers in waves based on:
- Device type (balancing iOS/Android)
- Province (representing all regions)
- Disability type (diverse perspectives)
- Tech experience (mix of novice and power users)

**Everyone who signs up will get access eventually** - earlier signups get priority.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I find a critical bug?</strong></summary>

**Report it immediately!** We have multiple ways:
1. In-app bug reporter (Settings → Help → Report Bug)
2. Email: [empowrapp08162025@gmail.com](mailto:empowrapp08162025@gmail.com)
3. Beta tester emergency hotline (provided in welcome email)

Critical bugs get fixed within 24-48 hours.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Will my beta testing data be kept after launch?</strong></summary>

**YES!** Your account, data, and progress will transition seamlessly to the full release. You won't lose anything.

**Exception:** If you choose to delete your account during beta, that's permanent.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I use my real data during beta?</strong></summary>

**Absolutely!** The app is production-ready with:
- Military-grade encryption (AES-256)
- Secure cloud backups (optional)
- BYOC modes (your data never touches our servers if you choose Strict mode)

However, if you're cautious, feel free to use dummy data initially and switch to real data once comfortable.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I'm not tech-savvy?</strong></summary>

**Perfect!** We NEED non-technical testers. Your perspective is invaluable.

We provide:
- Step-by-step setup guides
- Video tutorials
- Live onboarding sessions
- 1-on-1 support if needed

If you can send an email, you can beta test 3mpwrApp.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What happens if I stop using the app during beta?</strong></summary>

**Nothing bad!** There's no penalty. Life happens, especially in the disability community.

- Your account stays active
- Your data stays secure
- You can return anytime
- No pressure to participate

We understand spoons are limited. Use the app when you can.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can I share the app with friends?</strong></summary>

**During Closed Beta:** Please ask them to sign up via the waitlist (fair to everyone)

**During Open Beta (Q1 2026):** Yes! Share freely.

**After Launch (Q2 2026):** Absolutely! Spread the word.

</details>
