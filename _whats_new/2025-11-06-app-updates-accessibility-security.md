What's new in the 3mpwrApp:

**Accessibility Enhancements:**
- Dyslexia support with 5 font options (OpenDyslexic, Lexend, Arial, Helvetica, system default)
- 8 colored reading overlays for Irlen syndrome support
- Interactive features: tap words to highlight, drag reading ruler
- 14 screens now have dyslexia-friendly support
- Motor disability features: dwell-click technology (hold for 1-5 seconds, no tap needed)
- Visual progress indicator for dwell-click activation
- Increased touch targets (64x64pt) for easier tapping
- Tremor compensation to reduce accidental activations
- One-handed mode for left or right hand use

**Security & Privacy:**
- Military-grade AES-256 encryption for all your data
- Hardware-backed key storage (secure as possible)
- Complete offline operation (air-gapped mode)
- Optional private cloud support for organizations
- Network security with TLS 1.3 and certificate pinning
- Runtime integrity monitoring and anti-tampering protection
- Comprehensive input validation to prevent attacks
- Minimal permissions requested—privacy-first design

**Features Promoted to Beta:**
- Advocacy AI tools (Translator, Case interpreter, Gov Navigator, Policy simplifier)
- Wellness trackers (Symptom tracker, Pain forecast, Energy coins, Daily planner)
- Resources tools (Evidence locker, Chronic condition tracker)

Why it matters: We're making the app usable for people with dyslexia and motor disabilities, protecting your health data with enterprise-level security, and ensuring all your information stays under your control—even offline.
